Keynote Speaker: Ben Shneiderman

Hubble Telescope for Community Activity: Focusing on Social Media Visual Analytics

The availability of social media data presents remarkable opportunities and challenges for visual analytics researchers. The capacity to search, filter, analyze, and visualize real-time streams enables users to find patterns, relationships, and anomalies. Business and marketing professionals can track commentaries about their products, while political analysts can take the pulse of public sentiment. Among the many challenges is to filter the data to focus on relevant items in the context of adversaries who wish to influence public perceptions. Integrating statistical methods, text analytics, and visualization is essential, but creating compelling images with meaningful commentaries will keep researchers busy for decades.

Biography

Attendee Fast Forward

This session will take place at the beginning of the workshop and will allow any participant of the workshop to freely address the attendees for one minute. The session is free form and participants are encouraged to state their background, goals, and topics of interest with respect to visualization and social media. This event is an opportunity to understand the interests of all attendees and provides a venue to introduce directions of research. This session, at the Interactive Visual Text Analytics for Decision Making workshop, was vibrant and was an excellent way to encourage participant interaction.

Karen Wade Humanities Institute Ireland (University College Dublin)

Visualizing Online Communities: Applications from a literary and cultural studies perspective

Online social media texts, and the communities they both form and represent, present new methodological challenges to researchers in the arts and humanities, requiring reassessments of concepts such as author, audience and text, as well as new methods of textual selection and analysis. This talk aims to briefly outline ways in which visualization and network analysis techniques are currently being used to approach such challenges in humanities and social sciences research, and to provide an overview of an ongoing humanities-based research project utilizing visualization techniques, based on a collaboration between representatives from UCD's Clique Research Cluster and the Humanities Institute of Ireland.

Data scientists at Twitter strive each day to derive insights from petabytes of data in our Hadoop-based data warehouse.
